Our commitments

We create high quality pieces with durable materials without compromising aesthetics.

Transparency

We believe there is no perfect material; no magical solution when it comes to manufacturing accessories. With leather, as with alternatives, there are pros and cons, on which it is up to consumers to decide.

For those who are comfortable with buying animal products, our commitment is simple: for each product, we provide all the information required to make an informed choice.
